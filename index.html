About Me

This project involved the complete design and development of a custom Printed Circuit Board (PCB) from concept to final layout. I designed the schematic by carefully selecting components based on performance, power efficiency, and cost optimization. Special attention was given to signal integrity, trace width calculation, grounding techniques, and proper component placement to ensure stable and reliable circuit operation. The PCB was designed using industry-standard design practices, including proper layer stack-up planning, decoupling capacitor placement, and minimizing electromagnetic interference (EMI). I implemented clean routing techniques, optimized trace paths, and maintained appropriate clearances to meet manufacturing standards. Additionally, I performed Design Rule Checks (DRC) and Electrical Rule Checks (ERC) to verify the integrity of the circuit before fabrication. Thermal management considerations were also applied to prevent overheating of high-power components. This project strengthened my understanding of circuit analysis, hardware debugging, and PCB layout optimization. It demonstrates my ability to translate theoretical circuit designs into practical, manufacturable hardware solutions.
